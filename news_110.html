Firestrike: Mentorship Rewards

December 23, 2016December 21, 2016

Did you know? Players can earn up to 10,000 Gold, multiple 7-Day shields, and upgrade-boosting items with the new Mentorship system.

How mentorship works

  1. Click any +Add button at the bottom of your screen to view the Mentorship menu.
  2. Click Get Invite Link to receive your personal invite link. Click the code to copy it to the clipboard.
  3. Use Ctrl-V to paste the invite link where your friends can see it – for example, into a Facebook post, chat, or email.
  4. You’ll get notified as soon as anyone uses the invite link to join Firestrike.
  5. Rewards for both you, the inviter, and the new trainee unlock immediately when the trainee reaches Tactical Ops Center Level 3.
  6. Rewards increase as you gain more trainees. Check the Mentorship menu for details!

Ready to command your base? Play Firestrike now!

How to Customize Your Battlehouse Name

December 23, 2016December 23, 2016

When you first join Battlehouse, you might find your Battlehouse account name set to something like john.678.

You can choose your own custom name at this link: Battlehouse Account Settings.

Choose carefully – you can only set a custom name once. After that, it’s permanent.

Your Battlehouse account name applies to the Forums, Clan HQ, and games hosted on Battlehouse.com, such as FIRESTRIKE.

For now, games hosted on Facebook.com, such as THUNDER RUN and DAYS OF VALOR, still use the Call Sign you set within the game.

Thunder Run Battle Replay Contest

December 22, 2016December 23, 2016

Want to win a FREE Unit or Turret Blueprint of your choosing? Post a link to your most action-packed Battle Replay as a comment on our Thunder Run Forums, Thunder Run Facebook Page, or Thunder Run Twitter to be entered into a drawing to receive a free blueprint of your choosing.

Finalists who showcase the most entertaining and action-packed Battle Replay will be selected by our Community Team and announced on the forums on Friday, December 30th. From there, a poll will be created on the forums where you, the members of the Battlehouse Community, can vote on who you feel deserves to take home the Grand Prize!

Good hunting, Commanders!

NOTE: To post a Battle Replay link, click the “B” icon at the top of your game to open your “Battle Log”, click “View Log” next to the battle you want to share, followed by “View Replay”. Once the replay loads, click the “Get Link” button and paste the link to your desired social media platform (see Forums, Facebook, or Twitter links above).
