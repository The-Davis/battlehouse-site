DV: Elite Machine Gunner L2 Unit Equipment, and more – January 21st Patch Notes

January 21, 2021January 22, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Elite Machine Gunner L2 Unit Equipment

Gundar Arms Industries is rolling out more upgradeable weapons technologies. Purchase more Level 1 equipment sets in the ONP Store, and combine the parts in the Weapons Factory to make all new Level 2 sets! This week the Elite Machine Gunners are getting some new gear.

  • Elite Machine Gunner Laser Sights II – AN/PEQ-2 IR Laser Sight technology that increases range by 5%
  • Elite Machine Gunner Ballistic Armor MK II – Improved kevlar that reduces damage taken by 10%
  • Elite Machine Gunner GMC AP Ammunition II – The newest grade armor-piercing ammunition that increases damage by 10%
  • Also adds set bonuses to give Elite Machine Gunners the 80% FASCAM shielding that the old shield equipment set had.

Collect the entire Level 2 Unit Equipment set to boost your Unit Equipment bonuses even higher!

“Friends Bar” Changes

Due to the changes in the Facebook API, all friend recruitment quests haven’t been working as intended. We’ve decided to disable these quests. If you completed one of these quests, you should see an achievement in the Legacy category:

“Congrats on being such a friendly person!”

For all Facebook games: you will no longer see the friend bar if you have no Facebook friends in game. If you still have the friend bar, you will no longer be prompted or be able to add new friends, but you can still send & receives gifts to the friends that you already have.

  • Firestrike is not affected as it primarily uses the Battlehouse login system.
  • Thunder Run players using the Kongregate platform are not affected.
  • Battlefront Mars players using the Battlehouse login system are not affected.

In the future, we do plan on bringing back the “friends” bar, but more as a “clan bar.” We’re open to player feedback on how they’d like to see this implemented.

If you have any suggestions about this, or any general feedback you’d like to share, please let us know over the “days-of-valor” channel within our Thunder Run Discord Server.

To see where the design team currently stands on various Community suggested features and game improvements, check out the Days of Valor Voice of the Commander Tracker (tracker will receive some clean-up and updates to formatting in the very near future).

We have some exciting news, announcements, and more waiting for you on our most recent edition of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

Good hunting, Commanders!

DV: End of Season 16 Clan Tournament

January 1, 2021January 1, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

End of Season 16 Clan Tournament

🎇 Happy New Year, Commanders! 🎇

From yesterday December 31st, 2020 – January 7th, 2021, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 16!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

DV: Voice of the Commander Updates, Hardware bundle sale fix, and more – December 31st Patch Notes

December 31, 2020February 11, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Voice of the Commander Updates

We’re celebrating with a quality-of-life update based on your player feedback that’s intended to address a few of the issues and suggestions that you’ve brought forth to us via the “Voice of the Commander” trackers. Don’t see the update you were hoping for in this week’s release? Fear not, as there is still plenty more to come.

Deactivate your mines

We have heard your feedback loud and clear, and now, it is possible to disable minefields.

You can now disable your minefields during single-player AI attacks if you wish. This will automatically remove itself if you log out, or can be removed any time you want by clicking the status indicator icon and choosing “Remove” or clicking “Manage” on any mine and then clicking “Enable Minefields”

Known issue: When building new mines, the “Disable/Enable mine” button overlaps with the progress bar. This will be fixed really soon.

Hardware bundle bug fix and extension

Some players have pointed out that they were not getting the hardware bundle Christmas sale that started running last week. Our team has identified the issue and has pushed a hotfix that should fix it for all players within 24 hours.

Not only that, we’ll be extending the hardware bundle sale for one more week!

M4A1 Transport and Elite M4A1 Transport Updates

In preparation for customizable loadouts that we’ll be rolling out in the near future, we’ve made small changes to the M4A1 Transport and Elite M4A1 Transport units.

Particularly, the M4A1 Transport will have its special troops default back to stock Riflemen, and the Elite M4A1 Transport units are defaulted to Elite Riflemen. The Elite M4A1 Transport has also been given a slight damage increase.

Some may see this as a nerf, but don’t fret, commander! Customizable loadouts are coming and it’ll be worth it!

Upgrade/Unlock mouse-wheel support

And that’s not all, Commander! Upgrade and unlock windows now support mouse-wheel scrolling. No more clicking on the arrows on the top left of those windows!

DV: Voice of the Commander Updates – December 17th Patch Notes

December 17, 2020February 11, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Voice of the Commander Updates

We’re celebrating with a quality-of-life update based on your player feedback that’s intended to address a few of the issues and suggestions that you’ve brought forth to us via the “Voice of the Commander” trackers. Don’t see the update you were hoping for in this week’s release? Fear not, as there is still plenty more to come.

Acoustic Mine balance tweaks

We have heard your feedback loud and clear, and made some balance tweaks to the Acoustic Mines. They now have the following effects:

  • Stun now affects infantry and vehicles instead of just infantry
  • Damage debuff now affects all unit types instead of just infantry.
  • Range debuff now affects all unit types instead of just infantry and vehicles.
  • Speed debuff now affects infantry as well as vehicles.

Game improvements

And that’s not all, Commander! We made some tweaks to the UI as well! Any “On Impact” effects that have either 0 power or 0 duration at the current level or next level will not show the stat any longer.

The hover tooltip text for On Impact text will now list target types it has no effect against. Taking the Acoustic Mine’s changes above, for example, it now shows which targets its doesn’t have any effect on, if any.
