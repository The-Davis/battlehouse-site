TR: Elite MSTA Paint! – April 21st Patch Notes

April 21, 2022April 20, 2022

New this week in Thunder Run:

Attention, Commanders!

The Elite MSTA, gets its new colors this week!

To get them, simply head on over to the Store! You can either go to Store>Cosmetics to purchase them with gold; or go to “Event Prizes” to purchase them with ONP/Hardware.

Friendly reminder that only “basic” skins can be purchased with ONP/Hardware. Premium skins, such as black (and more to come in the future) can only be bought with gold. And of course, red and green skins are free!

Once bought, just activate the skins and head on over to your Motor Pool and choose your new coat of paint! Click “Apply Paint“, and you’re done!

We know what you’re thinking: why only the MSTA this week? Due to the massive amounts of paint we’ve acquired for several elite units in the past few weeks, our suppliers have started to run a little dry. Unfortunately, we don’t have enough paint for some other elite units, but we at least can cover two more vehicles in the next two weeks!

In response, we have requisitioned some new paint colors for the existing units instead. We know you hate the word “soon” so we’re not going to say it. But it is coming!

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over on our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Want to be Patron and get early access to future releases? Head on over to our Patreon!

Good hunting, Commanders!

BFM: Lightning Cannons are Online – April 21st Patch Notes

April 21, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in Battlefront Mars:

Lightning Cannon

The Lightning Cannon is here, and is now available for deployment to your Turret Platforms. Lightning Cannons fire disruptive energy beams at enemy aircraft and projectile weapons like missiles. To unlock it, visit the “Weapon Blueprints” section of the Event Prizes Store to obtain the new blueprints, and then unlock the new tech in your Weapons Lab.

DV: Welcome to Season 22!

April 14, 2022April 13, 2022

New this week in Days of Valor:

Welcome to Season 22!

Welcome to Season 22! Going into the new season, all Leaderboards and PvP Points are reset, meaning that it’s a fresh start for YOUR Clan to be the new the dominating Clan of Gundar, and win the Seasonal Clan Tournament Grand Prize of 10,000 Gold for your Clan at the end of the Season in 3 months from now. Does your Clan have what it takes?

New Batch of Elite Unit Skins

And that’s not all, Commanders! The next batch of elite unit skins are here!

The Elite Stinger Gunner, Elite M26 Pershing, and the Elite Harbin Z-9W get their skins this week!

To get them, simply head on over to the Store! You can either go to Store>Cosmetics to purchase them with gold; or go to “Event Prizes” to purchase them with ONP/Hardware.

Friendly reminder that only “basic” skins can be purchased with ONP/Hardware. Premium skins, such as black (and more to come in the future) can only be bought with gold. And of course, red and green skins are free!

New “Edit Scenery UI” Component

And that’s not all, Commanders! We’ve added new button that allows you to interact with placed scenery items on your base. You can find it at the right side of the screen, just below the “Zoom” buttons.

Clicking the icon switches you from “Edit Scenery” to “Editing Scenery” and back.

You can only interact with scenery items when it is on the “Editing Scenery” state. You can also manage your base in this state, but may find moving troops around difficult. So please make sure the icon is on “Edit Scenery” state when you are not fiddling around with scenery items.

Looking for the Season 21 Tournament winners list? Don’t worry. It will be revealed soon. Stay tuned for another Patch Notes coming later on!
