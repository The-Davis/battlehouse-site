Mars Frontier: Alliance Speedups – BETA Feature

December 30, 2017

New this week in Mars Frontier:

Alliance Help Speedups

Help your Alliance complete upgrades faster using the new BETA “Get Help” feature.

To get started, select “Speed Up” on any building upgrade and choose the new “Get Help” option.

Your request will appear in the Alliance chat window. Once 10 Alliance members respond by clicking your request, you will receive a speedup equal to 10% of the total upgrade time, or 10 minutes, whichever is greater.

Limitations:

  • You can ask for help once per hour.
  • Each upgrade can receive one help speedup.
  • Allies must be in the same Map to respond to your request.
  • There are no partial speed-ups. You must get 10 Allies to respond in order to receive a benefit.

Good hunting, commanders!

TR: New Elite Unit Improvements – December 28 Patch Notes

December 28, 2017December 28, 2017

New this week in Thunder Run:

Elite Units Gain Toughness

The following units now feature 20-50% greater HP at all levels:

  • Sapper
  • Elite Stryker
  • Elite Truck Bomb
  • Elite BRDM-3
  • Elite MSTA
  • Elite BTR-90
  • Elite TOS-1A
  • Elite Stinger Gunner
  • Elite Truck Bomb

And one training improvement:

  • Elite BRDM-3 now trains and repairs 5-10% faster at all levels

Technical Updates

  • Non-combat boost items like Fuel/Supply production boosts no longer take up space on the in-combat item hotbar.
  • Fixed a few cases where battles would not end automatically even after all enemy units and buildings were destroyed.
  • Various non-boost effects such as missile/CAS cooldowns were incorrectly counting against the player boost limit; these are now excluded from the limit.

Good hunting, Commanders!

Rare turret blueprints up to Level 12:

  • FASCAM

TR: Boost Your Defenses With “Eager” Security Teams

December 15, 2017December 16, 2017

New this week in Thunder Run:

Security teams from Level 4 leaders now deploy as soon as enemies approach a protected building! If the attacker retreats, the security team automatically returns back inside the building.

This update means that your base will be better protected once your leaders are upgraded to Level 4.

The first Level 4 teams to be available are:

  • Lima Force Turret Emplacement security teams and
  • Mike Force Hardware Depot/Leader Building security teams

To get started upgrading your leaders, check out the complete guide to high-level leaders on the Battlehouse forums.

And don’t forget, the Renegade event is updated with more ONP loot this week!

TR: Elite Truck Bomb Update

December 9, 2017December 6, 2017

Updated this week in Thunder Run:

Elite Truck Bomb: The Turret Killer

Several patches over the past few weeks have added more anti-turret power to the Elite Truck Bomb.

Here is a summary of the latest stats:

  • Large HP pool: up to 6,800 HP at max level
  • Extra DPS: the Elite Truck Bomb deals +50% extra damage against Turret Emplacements and other turret targets, over and above its listed DPS
  • PLUS, new this week: the Elite Truck Bomb now takes 70% less damage from turret weapon shots (up from 50% previously)

Have you given the Elite Truck Bomb a try yet? Good hunting, Commanders!
