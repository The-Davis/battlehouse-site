TR: Level 11 Tactical Ops Center, New Unit Equipment – August 9th Patch Notes

August 9, 2018August 9, 2018

New this week in Thunder Run:

Tactical Ops Center Level 11

Your architects have been hard at work, Commander. In addition to a sharply-worded requisition order for more coffee, they’ve delivered blueprints for the next Tactical Ops Center expansion. It seems like only yesterday we were huddled together in tents.

Tick-Tock! The TOC now upgrades to Level 11, unlocking a variety of new upgrades for your military forces!

New features include:

  • Increased level cap on your resource production and storage facilities.
  • Enables increased level cap on Howitzer, C-RAM, HEL, FASCAM, and Quadmount turret mounts. Available in the store now!
  • Enables increased level cap on infantry research and production buildings. Coming soon!
  • Enables increased level cap on aircraft research and production buildings. Coming soon!

You’ll need a lot of resources to upgrade your Tactical Operations Center to level 11. Better beef up your security teams so your enemies don’t sniff around for easy prey.

Elite M109A7 Unit Equipment & Unit Stat Upgrades

Gundar Arms Industries is rolling out a new set of Unit Equipment for the Elite M109A7, available this week on the front page of the Event Prizes Store:

  • Elite M109A7 Laser Sights I – Laser homing technology that increase range by +3%
  • Elite M109A7 Ballistic Armor MK I – Improved armor coating reduces damage taken by -10%
  • Elite M109A7 Ammunition I – The newest grade armor-piercing ammunition increases damage by +7%

In addition to the new Unit Equipment, the Elite M109A7 also now features individually-upgradable Health and Damage stats, similar to other elite units.

  • These Level 6 stat upgrades require Unit Stat Blueprints, available for ONP in a new section of the Event Prizes store.

New Building Levels

To help support all of this new technology, upgrades are also now available for the following resource storage buildings:

  • Suppy Depots are now upgradeable to Level 16.
  • Fuel Depots are now upgradeable to Level 16.
  • Hardware Depots are now upgradeable to Level 11.

Macgyver7th Livestream

Want to see a live demo of the TOC11 in action? Come watch Battlehouse Stream Team Leader Macgyver7th show off this week’s updates for us on his YouTube channel!

Good hunting!

BFM: Daily Battle Replay Sharing Rewards – August 2nd Patch Notes

August 2, 2018August 2, 2018

New in Battlefront Mars:

Daily Battle Replay Sharing Rewards

Claim a free Player Appreciation Loot Pack every day by sharing a replay of one of your top battles to your Facebook timeline.

After a battle ends: from the “post battle summary” screen, select the loot chest over the “Share Replay” button and follow the prompts to post the replay to your Facebook timeline.

You can claim one free gift using this method every day, so be sure to get into the habit of sharing your top replays daily!
