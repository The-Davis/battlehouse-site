TR: Heavy Anti-Tank Mines Now Available

May 24, 2018May 24, 2018

New this week in Thunder Run:

Gundar arms industries have developed a Heavy Anti-Tank Mine that will only detonate when approached by the heaviest ground vehicles.

Unlike the standard Anti-Tank Mine, this heavy version cannot be triggered prematurely by light vehicles such as HMMWVs or Strykers.

To unlock the new mines, visit your local Weapons Lab and open the Unlock & Upgrade Menu!

Vehicle Classification

To check whether a unit will trigger Heavy Anti-Tank Mines, look for the word “Light” or “Heavy” under the unit’s name in the Army → Train menu. Light units will not set off Heavy Anti-Tank Mines, but heavy units will.

TR: Syndicate Loot Update, Level 4 India and Juliett Leaders – May 17 Patch Notes

May 17, 2018May 15, 2018
patch_notes_tr

New this week in Thunder Run:

New Level 4 Leaders

This week, two new Level 4 leader teams are available:

  • India Force leaders that defend Fuel Depots and
  • Juliett Force leaders that defend Generators

Security teams from Level 4 leaders deploy as soon as enemies approach a protected building. If the attacker retreats, the security team automatically returns back inside the building.

This update means that your base will be better protected once your leaders are upgraded to Level 4.

To get started upgrading your leaders, check out the complete guide to high-level leaders on the Battlehouse forums.

Syndicate Loot Update

In this week’s special event, all Syndicate single-player levels now give double ONP loot and double Fuel/Supply loot.

Plus, on the map, all Rusla bases now contain 15% more ONP loot and 25% more Fuel/Supply loot.

These updates bring the risk/reward balance for the Syndicate event more in line with other Thunder Run special events. Previously, Syndicate bases gave less ONP, Fuel, and Supply when compared with other events at similar difficulty levels.

TR: Renegade Battle Replay Contest

May 4, 2018

Starting today in Thunder Run:

Think you’ve got what it takes to prove you’re the Master of the Renegade Event? Post a link to your most action-packed, skilled, and creative Renegade Event Battle Replay as a comment on this post on our Thunder Run Facebook Page, no later than Friday, May 11th at 17:00 GMT to become eligible to win BIG prizes! üí∞

Submission Guidelines

Your Renegade Battle Replay will be graded by the Battlehouse Community Team on 3 base criteria, all graded on a scale on 1-5. Additional bonus points can be earned for following certain guidelines:

3 Primary Grading Criteria:

  • Action (1-5 pts.)
  • Skill (1-5 pts.)
  • Creativity (1-5 pts.)

Bonus Point Allowances:

  • +10 pts. for the replay being a Single Player Renegade Event Level or Karnoff ONP Base
  • +10 pts. for sharing a Home Base defense (not an attack) that features FASCAM Turrets
  • +5 pts. for featuring the TOS-1A (25% ONP discount this week)
  • +5 pts. for featuring the Elite KA-50 (25% ONP discount this week)
  • +5 pts. for recording your replay and hosting it on YouTube, Twitch, or Facebook Watch

The players who score the highest will be ranked on a Leaderboard, and announced on the forums on Friday, May 11th. Please only one entry per player! If you submit multiple replay entries, only your first entry will be graded. We will also not grade entries submitted by the same player via an alt account.

NOTE: To post a Battle Replay link, click the “B” icon at the top of your game to open your “Battle Log”, click “View Log” next to the battle you want to share, followed by “View Replay”. Once the replay loads, click the “Get Link” button and paste the link as a comment on this post on our Thunder Run Facebook Page.

Prizes

  • 1st Place: 200,000 ONP (and your replay will be featured on our Battlehouse YouTube channel)
  • 2nd Place: 100,000 ONP
  • 3rd Place: 75,000 ONP
  • 4th – 10th Place: 50,000 ONP
