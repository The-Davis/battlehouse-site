TR: Quarry Turret Upgrades, More Unit Improvements – November 9 Patch Notes

patch_notes_tr
November 9, 2017November 8, 2017

New this week in Thunder Run:

New Levels

  • The Transmitter can now be upgraded to Level 10, allowing more and bigger units to be donated to Clanmates.

Unit Improvements

  • Elite Rifleman: 30% more HP at all levels. Now sports up to 8,750 HP at max level!
  • Elite Truck Bomb is now specialized as a turret-killer:
    • +300% damage against Turret Emplacements and MG/Mortar/TOW Towers
    • -50% damage against targets that are not turrets

Quarry Turret Upgrades

Inside of quarries, MG Towers, Mortar Emplacements, and TOW Emplacements can now be upgraded to boost defenses.

The maximum upgrade level depends on the size of the quarry, as follows:

  • Small (S) Quarries: MG Tower L4, Mortar Emplacement L4
  • Medium (M) Quarries: MG Tower L6, Mortar Emplacement L6
  • Large (L) Quarries: MG Tower L8, Mortar Emplacement L8, TOW Emplacement L2
  • Extra-Large (XL) Quarries: MG Tower L10, Mortar Emplacement L10, TOW Emplacement L4

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Units Powering Up – November 2 Patch Notes

November 2, 2017November 3, 2017

New this week in Thunder Run:

Elite Truck Bomb Update

The Elite Truck Bomb gains two improvements to make it more effective for killing Turret Emplacements:

  • Double HP at all levels
  • Anti-turret armor: the Elite Truck Bomb now takes 25% less damage from turret shots
    • Displayed as a new Turret Armor stat on the Upgrade & Unlock menu

Other Unit Improvements

More units are getting improved this week:

  • Elite Rifleman:
    • 20% more DPS
    • 50% more damage vs. aircraft
    • 15% more weapon range
  • Elite OH-58: 15% more DPS
  • Combat Engineer: 10% lower training time
  • Truck Bomb: 20% more HP at all levels
  • Elite MSTA: 25% more HP at all levels
  • MQ-8B: 20% more HP at all levels

New Equipment Items

This week’s special event also features new Event Prizes:

  • AP Ammunition, Ballistic Armor, Laser Sights for the following units:
    • Elite Stryker
    • Elite OH-58

Technical Updates

  • When a friendly unit drops below 50% health, the HP bar now changes color from green to yellow (and enemy HP bars change to a darker shade of red).
  • As announced previously, the maximum XP level is now Level 75.

Good hunting, Commanders!

The Community has spoken, and we hear you loud and clear! Coming October 23rd in Thunder Run and Days of Valor:

PvP Updates

Starting on October 23rd

Landmine Production Bonuses, Turret Sale, and More! – October 19 Patch Notes

October 19, 2017October 19, 2017

New this week in Thunder Run and Days of Valor:

Building Improvements

The Level 11+ Weapons Factory now gives greater bonuses to production speed of landmines and turret weapons. The speed bonus is now +85% for a Level 14 Weapons Factory.

The Level 9+ Leader Barracks now gives a greater bonus to leader training speed. The bonus is now +210% for a Level 10 Leader Barracks.

Unit Updates

Several blueprint-based units are increasing in toughness. The following units now have 20-30% greater HP at all levels:

  • BRDM-3
  • Elite OH-58
  • Elite Rifleman
  • Truck Bomb
  • MQ-8B

Security teams will no longer activate from units that begin a battle with less than 50% HP. To deploy a security team when a unit is destroyed, the unit must start the battle with at least 50% HP.

Event Prizes Turret Blueprint Sale

This week only – the Event Prizes store includes big ONP discounts on defensive turret blueprints:

Level 11-12 blueprints:

  • FASCAM
  • M45 Quadmount
  • C-RAM
  • HEL Laser
  • Artillery

Level 11-12 and Level 13-14 blueprints:

  • MG Tower
  • Mortar
  • TOW Missile Launcher

Clan Membership History Improvements

  • The Clan History tab on the Player Info menu now shows the name of the Clan officer responsible for inviting or kicking the player.

Good hunting, commanders!

All blueprint-based units now feature upgradable Mine Shield stats. Unlock this stat to Level 3 to add 15% damage resistance against landmines, meaning that units will take 15% less damage than usual when hit by mine blasts.
