WSE: Welcome to Season 12!

January 9, 2020July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Welcome to Season 12!

Welcome to Season 12! Going into the new season, all Leaderboards and PvP Points are reset, meaning that it’s a fresh start for YOUR Clan to be the new the dominating Clan of the Season, and win the Seasonal Clan Tournament Grand Prize of 10,000 Gold for your Clan at the end of the Season in 3 months from now. Does your Clan have what it takes?

For a sneak peak at what’s coming up this season in Battlehouse games, check out the Developer Roadmap in the most recent edition of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

Game Improvements

We’ve also made another high in community demand quality-of-life improvement to the settings menu interface!

The immersion based “screen shake” effect that occurs after a building is destroyed is now optional, so players who are sensitive to the screen movement or players that experience additional game lag from the effects can turn them off if they choose to do so.

Head on into the settings menu, scroll down to the new “Enable Screen Shake” preference, toggle it over to “No”, and then hit “Apply” if you want to turn the effects off at any point.

To see where the design team currently stands on various Community suggested features and game improvements, check out the War Star Empire Voice of the Commander Tracker.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

WSE: End of Season 11 Clan Tournament

January 2, 2020July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

End of Season 11 Clan Tournament

🎆 Happy New Year, Commanders! 🎇 From January 2nd – January 9th, 2020, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 11!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on January 9th, 2020 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 12 opens on January 9th, 2020!

WSE: Central Computer Level 9, and more – December 26th Patch Notes

December 26, 2019July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Central Computer Level 9

🎄 Happy Holidays, Commanders! 🎄

We’ve been hearing your feedback loud and clear, and we’re ready to celebrate the holidays on Mars with the mega update you’ve all been patiently waiting for. Effective today:

The Central Computer level cap is raised to Level 9, paving the way for new maximum levels for your production, storage, and research buildings!

Game Improvements

And that’s not all! We’ve also made some improvements to the settings menu interface!

  • The settings menu is now scrollable, allowing for more player preference options.
  • A new setting option now gives players the ability to toggle the mousewheel scrolling behavior for in-game chat, and regional map zooming.
  • Soon more setting options will be added that allow players to toggle screen shaking effects, and in-game chat window orientation. Stay tuned!

Like what you see? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our TR Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

WSE: Level 12 Warehouse and Reserves Bay – October 31st Patch Notes

December 12, 2019July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

New Unit Level Upgrades

The Mars Coalition made a technology breakthrough that enables us the ability to research new levels for the Shock Trooper, Tracker, Avenger, and Gun Runner.

Effective now:

  • Shock Troopers, Trackers, Avengers, and Gun Runners each get 3 additional levels, and are now upgradeable to L11.

Even better, one of our recon rovers is reporting that Central Computer Level 9 and more are just around the corner. Read more in the latest issue of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

Bug Fixes

  • The L11 LRAT research requirement was previously incorrectly showing as needing Central Computer Level 9. It has been updated to it’s intended level requirement of CCL8.
  • An error message that was appearing when viewing certain turret-related missions is now fixed.

Like what you see, or have a favorite unit that you’d like to see new levels added for down the road? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our TR Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

WSE: Level 12 Warehouse and Reserves Bay – October 31st Patch Notes

October 31, 2019July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Level 12 Warehouse/Reserves Bay

Happy Halloween, Commanders! We’re hearing your community feedback loud and clear. Last week as part of our weekly “War Star Empire Voice of the Commander” sweep, a few of you expressed to us that you want more Warehouse space, as well as an increase in Army Capacity from the Reserves Bay. Guess what? We’re here to deliver. We may not have any candy to hand out to all of you this Halloween, but more Warehouse space and Army Capacity is even better! Effective today:

  • The Warehouse is now upgradeable to L12 for players who have Central Computer L8, and all existing Warehouse levels have been given a buff to warehouse space.
  • The Reserves Bay is now upgradeable to L11, allowing you to increase your army capacity!

WSE: Level 8 Central Computer Build Time Adjustments – August 22nd Patch Notes

August 22, 2019July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Level 8 Central Computer

Attention, Commanders! The Mars Coalition has been hearing your feedback loud and clear regarding the 90 day build time for the Level 8 Central Computer being too long, and they want to help!

Effective this week: the previous 90 day build time has been reduced to 12 days. To help maintain a proper balance and keep things fair for all players, the costs for upgrading remain the same as they were previously regardless of time spent. If you are currently building and show more than 12 days remaining, you may wish to cancel and restart the project. Waiting to build/buy until now is the same cost as building/buying at release.

Like what you see? Let us know over on Clan HQ.

Good hunting, Commanders!

WSE: Level 8 Central Computer, New Turret Levels – July 18th Patch Notes

July 18, 2019July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Level 8 Central Computer

Attention, Commanders! The Mars Coalition has made a huge technology breakthrough, and our Central Computer is now upgradeable to Level 8.

The new Central Computer level paves the way for new maximum levels, allowing you to upgrade most base buildings an additional 1 level higher. Included in the maximum level caps for buildings are newly expanded levels for the Turrets System:

  • Turret Platforms, Auto-Cannons, Thunder Cannons, and Devastators: now upgradeable to L14.
  • Radiation Towers, LRAT Cannons, and EMP Cannons: now upgradeable to L12
