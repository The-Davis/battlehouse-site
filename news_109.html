Firestrike Dev Note: Generators

December 31, 2016December 29, 2016

Generators supply vital power for base buildings and turrets in Firestrike.

A few players have asked about the reasons for the specific stats and levels on Generators. Here is the answer from the design team:

Each Tactical Ops Center (TOC) level unlocks a certain number of Generators, and allows them to be upgraded to a certain level limit. For example, TOC Level 9 allows 3 Generators of up to Level 9 each. The power stats of Generators are designed so that, for each TOC level, a fully-upgraded set of Generators provides just enough juice to power a maxed-out base with all the buildings built and upgraded to the limit for that TOC level. As long as you keep upgrading your Generators along with your other buildings, you should never need to run out of power.

Firestrike Hotfix: December 28, 2016

December 28, 2016

Attention FIRESTRIKE players:

  • Generators no longer require Fuel to upgrade to Level 6, and no longer require Hardware to upgrade to Level 11.

This fixes the situation where you reach Tactical Ops Center Level 6 and are unable to build a Fuel Depot or Fuel Yard due to lack of power.

Note, the Supply cost of Generator Level 6 was increased to compensate for the removal of a Fuel requirement. This keeps the designed challenge level for the upgrade the same as before.
