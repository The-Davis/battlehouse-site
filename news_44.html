TR: L16 Elite AH-64 and more – June 4th Patch Notes

June 4, 2020June 4, 2020

New this week in Thunder Run:

L16 Elite AH-64

The Elite AH-64 is here.

As with the recent max unit level increases, your Level 16 Elite AH-64 will feature improved stats and no increase in battalion space requirements. As with any improvement in power, there is an increase in cost, production time, and repair time.

The best part about it? No blueprints are required for upgrading. As long as you have TOC12 and the new building levels for your Maintenance Hangar and Airfield, you can upgrade away!

Craftable Strike Team Improvements

This week we’re also bringing you some quality-of-life updates for the new Craftable Strike Team system. Effective now:

  • Strike Team Letter-Number tags now appear when hovering over an Ambush Point in the home base view. Below that it will also now show the equipped Strike Team/Squadron and its level.
  • You can now view the stats of any mine, strike team, or strike squadron by clicking on them in the Strike Team Production Menu.

Mysterious riflemen, identified

You might have come across a mysterious group of riflemen in singleplayer maps. These units are part of an update to our cheat detection system. Don’t worry, Commander! If you see these units, it does NOT mean the system thinks you are cheating. It actually means you aren’t cheating.

Daily free repair kit giveaway status

The daily free repair kit giveaway is extended through Saturday, June 6th. Tomorrow, June 5th, we’ll announce whether it will continue for another week or not. Whenever it may end, we’re looking at giving away one Repair Kit and Free Repair Kit as part of the Day 7 Login Reward.

To see where the design team currently stands on various Community suggested features and game improvements, check out the Thunder Run Voice of the Commander Tracker (tracker will receive some clean-up and updates to formatting in the very near future).

We have some exciting news, announcements, and more waiting for you on our most recent edition of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

Have new suggestions or feedback to share? Let us know over on our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Rifleman L2 Unit Equipment, and Craftable Strike Teams – May 28th Patch Notes

May 28, 2020May 28, 2020

New this week in Thunder Run:

Rifleman L2 Unit Equipment

Gundar Arms Industries is rolling out more upgradeable weapons technologies. Purchase more Level 1 equipment sets in the ONP Store, and combine the parts in the Weapons Factory to make all new Level 2 sets!

  • Rifleman Laser Sights II – AN/PEQ-2 IR Laser Sight technology that increases range by 5%
  • Rifleman Ballistic Armor MK II – Improved kevlar that reduces damage taken by 10%
  • Rifleman GMC AP Ammunition II – The newest grade armor-piercing ammunition that increases damage by 10%
  • Also adds set bonuses to give Rifleman the 80% FASCAM shielding that the old shield equipment set had.

Collect the entire Level 2 Unit Equipment set to boost your Unit Equipment bonuses even higher!

Craftable Strike Teams

You can now craft your Strike Teams and Strike Squadrons from the Weapons Factory. Drop an ambush point on your base and choose which Strike Team to deploy on it!

  • All previously owned Strike Teams are now removed from your warehouses, and have been refunded in ONP during the week of May 14th – 23rd.
  • In the Minefield or Ambush Point management system, the stats of any item in the grid can be viewed by clicking on it.
  • Hovering over an ambush point shows the equipped Strike Team/Squadron and its level.

TR: L3 Base Perimeter Upgrade, Minefield Updates, and more – May 14th Patch Notes

May 14, 2020May 14, 2020

New this week in Thunder Run:

L3 Base Perimeter Upgrade

Looks like it’s getting a little crowded in your base, Commander. Good news! Gundar Arms Industries has secured a new deal on land charters, granting us the ability to purchase more land around our base perimeter.

  • Expand by your base perimeter by an additional 10%!
  • You must have at least Tactical Ops Center L9 to access the new expansion tier.

If you’re TOCL9 or above: the L3 Base Perimeter upgrade is available now, and accessible via the in-game “Defenses” Store for 1,430 Gold. 

Minefield Updates

Your engineers have improved their minefield charting system. They can now plant more than 28 mines without blowing off their own feet. All those years of playing Minesweeper paid off, I guess?

– TOC levels 3 through 12 all get a boost to the number of minefields they can deploy.
– TOC levels 3 through 10 can build two additional minefields.
– TOC level 11 can build 4 additional minefields, and TOC level 12 can build 6 additional minefields.

No additional research is needed to unlock the additional minefields. Just start building!

Game Improvements

  • While this week’s event store is open, from May 14 through May 23, players who previously purchased a Strike Team or Strike Squadron will have those purchases refunded. The items will no longer be purchasable in the store. The new crafting system will go live some time after this migration and refund process is complete.
  • Volume Controls update: clicking the volume bar between the plus and minus sign will now mute/unmute your volume. The UI tooltip is updated to reflect this. The game will save your last unmuted volume preference and restore it if you mute. Example: Set music volume to 50%. Click the yellow bar. It’s muted. Click the empty bar. It’s instantly back to 50%.
