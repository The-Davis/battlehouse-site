Battlefront Mars: End of Season 5 Clan Tournament

March 29, 2018March 29, 2018

This week Battlefront Mars features an End of Season Clan Tournament, followed by the opening of Season 6!

Season Finale Tournament

From March 29th – April 5th, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 5!

Tournament ends on April 5th, 2018 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season.

New Season Opens: April 5th

The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament. The following changes are planned:

Battlefront Mars (Season 6):

  • All players will be able to re-attack bases for PvP points every 6 hours 🎖
  • New Battle Music 🎶

Good hunting, Commanders!

Mars Frontier: End of Season 10 Alliance Tournament

March 29, 2018April 3, 2018

This week Mars Frontier features an End of Season Alliance Tournament, followed by the opening of Season 11!

Season Finale Tournament

From March 29th – April 5th, rally your Alliance and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Alliance of Season 10!

Tournament ends on April 5th, 2018 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season.

New Season Opens: April 5th

The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament. The following changes are planned:

Mars Frontier (Season 11):

  • One new map will be added: Outlands 21 🚁
  • Some old maps will be retired: Lowlands 1, 2, 3, and Outlands 3, 17, and 20 🌊
  • All players will be able to re-attack bases for PvP points every 6 hours 🎖
    • To be clear, this is not a change to DP or bubbling. This only means that PvP point gain/loss will be enabled again 6 hours after the last battle involving PvP points. DP bubbles still work exactly the same as before.
  • New Battle Music 🎶

Good hunting, Commanders!

    TR: Epidemic of Loot, Level 4 Echo and Foxtrot Leaders – March 22 Patch Notes

    March 22, 2018March 22, 2018

    New this week in Thunder Run:

    More Loot!

    In this week’s special event, all Epidemic single-player levels now give 75% more ONP loot.

    Plus, on the map, all Harding bases are now packed with 75% more ONP loot and 50% more Fuel/Supply loot.

    Earn up to 34,000 ONP, 2.3 million Fuel, and 2.3 million Supply from a full kill of a Harding L34 base!

    Map Base Fuel/Supply Loot ONP Loot
    Harding L26 1.2m/1.2m 16,000
    Harding L30 1.95m/1.95m 25,000
    Harding L34 2.32m/2.32m 34,000

    New Level 4 Leader Teams

    This week, two new Level 4 leader teams are available:

    • Echo Force leaders that boost infantry repair, training, and research speeds, plus
    • Foxtrot Force leaders that boost armor repair, training, and research speeds

    The Level 4 upgrade provides greater speed boosts than ever before!

    To get started upgrading your leaders, check out the complete guide to high-level leaders on the Battlehouse forums.

    Technical Update

    • Temporary boost items that apply to the OH-58 and Stryker now also apply to the Elite versions of these units, matching how other temporary boost items work.

    Good hunting, commanders!

    DV: Time Challenge Competition – Round 2

    March 21, 2018March 20, 2018

    In response to the overwhelming popularity of the new Time Challenge Competition in Days of Valor, we’re releasing a 2nd Time Challenge Level only accessible for the next 24 hours, along with a 2nd set of Leaderboard Rewards.

    Time Challenge Competition Details

    From March 15th – March 22nd, access the all new Time Challenge levels in the Hit List by going to Attack -> Hit List. Destroy the Tactical Ops Center in the fastest times possible to earn placement on the Time Challenge Leaderboards.

    Competition ends on March 22nd, 2017 at 17:00 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, and prizes awarded just in time for next week’s ONP Event.

    For a more in-depth list of Time Challenge rules, click HERE.

    Time Challenge Prizes

  • 1st Place: 100,000 ONP
  • 2nd Place: 75,000 ONP
  • 3rd Place: 50,000 ONP
  • 4th – 10th Place: 25,000 ONP

    • Note: Each Time Challenge level has it’s own set of identical prizes.
    Good hunting, Commanders!
