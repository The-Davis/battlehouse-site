MF: End of Season 23 Alliance Tournament – July 8th Patch Notes

July 8, 2021July 26, 2022

New this week in Mars Frontier:

End of Season 23 Alliance Tournament

From July 8th, 2021 – July 15th, 2021, rally your Alliance and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Alliance of Season 23!

Be one of the Top Alliances with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Alloy, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your Alliance.

Tournament ends on July 15th, 2021 at 1700 GMT at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season.

Season 24 Opens: July 15th, 2021

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Lowlands” maps.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “mars-frontier” channel within our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

WSE: End of Season 17 Clan Tournament, and more – July 8th Patch Notes

July 8, 2021July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

End of Season 17 Clan Tournament

Heads up, Martians!

From July 8th, 2021 – July 15th, 2021, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 17!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on July 15th, 2021 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 18 opens on July 15th, 2021, 2021

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Highlands” maps.

Game improvements

And that’s not all, Commanders! When you log in to an attacked base, you will now be given a prompt to rearm all your mines.

The “Rearm Mines” button will direct you to the store buy more resources if you don’t have enough to craft any of the mines. Otherwise, it will batch the crafting commands for you.

We know, we know. It’s another pop-up window. If you prefer to rearm your mines manually, you can turn this off in your player preferences.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

DV: Medic Mayhem Pack, and more – July 1st Patch Notes

July 1, 2021July 6, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Medic Mayhem Pack

Incoming supply drop, Commanders!

Introducing the “Medic Mayhem Pack!” From July 1st to July 5th, some gold offers come with repair kits!

Simply login and go to your Gold Menu to take advantage of two offers; one with two repair kits of each type, and one with four repair kits!

Game improvements

And that’s not all, Commander! If you’re tired of upgrading your defensive walls one by one, then this is for you: there’s now an option to upgrade all barriers with a single click!

Clicking “Upgrade All” will give you the option on whether to upgrade all barriers if your resources are enough, or to upgrade the entire base’s barriers using Gold.

If you like to do it the old-fashioned way and don’t see yourself using it, you may turn this option off in your game settings.

We have some exciting news, announcements, and more waiting for you on our most recent edition of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

TR: AI Missile Defense Nerfs, and more – June 24th Patch Notes

June 24, 2021June 23, 2021

New this week in Thunder Run:

AI Missile Defense Nerfs

Attention, Commanders!

We have reviewed AI battle data, and last week, have made some tweaks to the missile defenses of AI bases.

Our intent has always been for players to use a combination of missiles and units to conquer AI bases; not to spam missiles and win, and not to have missiles be useless. Recently, the latter appears more true.

The strongest AI missile defenses will now have 24% chance to shoot down a missile (down from 48%). More importantly, the number of buildings with missile defenses will be reduced to 1/4 of the turrets on TOC levels 1-5, and 1/3 of turrets on TOC levels 6-12.

Missile upgrades

And that’s not all, Commanders!

Our engineers just had a breakthrough on our missile technology, and some of them now deliver a little extra kick!

The EMP Missile level cap has been increased to Level 15, the EMP Missile Volley level cap has been increased to Level 14, and the High Explosive Missile level cap has been increased to Level 13!
