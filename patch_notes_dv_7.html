DV: Hardware Base Update, EMP Missile and Volley Upgrades, and more – June 10th Patch Notes

June 10, 2021June 9, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Hardware Base Updates

Attention, Commanders! Our spies have reported that our enemies have not taken kindly to their Hardware Depots being constantly sniped, and they have developed a countermeasure that allows their Hardware Depots to be impervious to attacks.

Fear not, Commander, for our intel suggests that these countermeasures are controlled within their Tactical Ops Center. Simply destroy their TOC, and their Hardware Depots become vulnerable again.

Starting June 10th, event bases will have their Hardware Depots “protected” until the TOC is destroyed. This is response to long-time feedback from players who pointed out that raiding Hardware and ignoring the TOC leads to the event base being left intact on the regional map, and thereby preventing new ones to spawn.

Security Node Bug Fix

A bug was discovered where Security Nodes only worked when players were defending against singleplayer defense missions. This has been fixed, and will now work everywhere. Expect fully-upgraded player bases to be much stronger in PvP fights soon.

Moar Hardware Rewards

The above two updates will no doubt come with additional challenges to event bases. Therefore, Hardware rewards on event bases have been increased by 25%. Challenge accepted, Commander?

EMP Missile and EMP Missile Volley upgrades

Attention, Commanders! Our engineers just had a breakthrough on our EMP Missiles, and they can now have that little extra kick to them. The EMP Missile level cap has been increased to Level 14, and the EMP Missile Volley level cap has been increased to Level 13!

DV: New Missile Silo and Craftable Missiles – May 6th Patch Notes

May 6, 2021May 5, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Craftable Missiles

Hey, Commanders! Ever get tired of stealing missiles from the bad guys? Don’t you wish you had a way to produce your own? Well, now you do!

Our engineers have made some breakthroughs and we now have the ability to research, produce, and launch our very own missiles!

To begin, research any missile from the Weapons Lab. This will unlock the Missile Silo, which you can construct from your Buildings>Production menu. From there, you may research any other missiles you wish to use, and craft them under the “Produce” tab.

Once crafted, you can use them in battle!

Upgrade your Missile Silo for more missile capacity, or upgrade your missiles for more damage and AoE. Note that some higher level missiles may require blueprints purchased from the ONP store to upgrade.

And that’s not all, Commander! Our engineers are also hard at work coming up with new missiles. In the future, we may see mine-busters, ambush/mine-detectors, or even missiles that can break us out of a siege!

Until then, rain hell upon your enemies, Commander!

DV: Welcome to Season 18! New “Valor March” Map, 75% sale, and more – April 1st Patch Notes

April 1, 2021April 1, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Welcome to Season 18!

Welcome to Season 18! Going into the new season, all Leaderboards and PvP Points are reset, meaning that it’s a fresh start for YOUR Clan to be the new the dominating Clan of Gundar, and win the Seasonal Clan Tournament Grand Prize of 10,000 Gold for your Clan at the end of the Season in 3 months from now. Does your Clan have what it takes?

New “Valor March” map

Get ready to March, Commander! This season, only the new “Valor March” map will be ladder-enabled. So if you want PvP Points, this is the map for you!

Valor March requires a TOC6 or higher to enter, and will be anti-alt and anti-VPN. There will also be no shields or damage protection. Should you move out of the map at any point, any accumulated PvP Points will be lost.

Valor awaits, Commander! Are you ready?

Please be reminded that the El Guettar map will be closed starting this season.

April Fool’s Sale!

“They said there’d never be a 75% off sale again… But surely we can sneak in a quick one for all of our favorite Commanders to celebreate April Fools Day, right?

From midnight April 1st to midnight April 2nd GMT, take advantage of this explosive 75%-gold sale! After April Fools, a normal 50%-off sale will remain available from midnight April 2nd to midnight April 5th.

This is not an April Fools joke! Or is it?”

Kongregate accounts news

Beginning April 1st, Kongregate accounts will be moved to Kongregate-only maps unless they fit one of the following criteria:

  • At least 6 months old
  • At least Level 6 TOC
  • Have bought gold

Please be aware this is not an April fools joke, but a response to Kongregate accounts being used for alt abuse.

DV: Voice of the Commander Updates – February 4th Patch Notes

February 4, 2021February 11, 2021

New this week in Days of Valor:

Voice of the Commander Updates

We’re celebrating with a quality-of-life update based on your player feedback that’s intended to address a few of the issues and suggestions that you’ve brought forth to us via the “Voice of the Commander” trackers. Don’t see the update you were hoping for in this week’s release? Fear not, as there is still plenty more to come.

Chat copy-to-clipboard

Chat messages have been updated. Was that last chat so funny you want to save it forever? Confused if someone using another language is talking trash or feeling friendly? All messages now have a “Copy to Clipboard” feature. You can paste into your favorite translation program or your secret repository of clan messages.

May the ability to translate chat messages bring more understanding and enjoyment of the game rather than less!

New “Repair Base” button

You may notice a new wrench button on your controls. This new repair base feature will allow you to bring back the repair for gold window if you chose “repair slowly”.

It will also inform you via tooltip/hovertext how much gold it will take to fully repair your base.

Best of all, once your buildings hit the free repair threshold, the wrench will turn green, indicating that you can repair all buildings for free with a single click. No more visiting each building in your base and clicking “speed up” when they hit five minutes.

Requirements for next level during upgrade

While upgrades or research are in progress, the resource requirements will now be shown in the Stats window, to allow players to manage their resources more effectively.

Hovering on “Requirements” confirms which level and costs are being shown for.

DV: Logistics Dispatch Upgrades – January 28th Patch Notes

January 28, 2021January 28, 2021

This weekend in Days of Valor:

Logistic Dispatch Upgrades

Hey Commanders! The Logistics Dispatch has received some upgrades! Starting at TOC L6, you can upgrade your Logistics Dispatch all the way to level 8.

Each new level of the Logistics Dispatch increases the dispatch speed and increases the size of the dispatch rewards. From L2+ the Logistics Dispatch can retrieve Hardware, though this is a very time-consuming operation.

And that’s not all, Commander! All dispatches now receive double the old rewards. Supply dispatches are now available more frequently.

The L2+ Logistics Dispatch center is available today, but you may have noticed that the increased rewards have been around for a couple of days.
