MF: End of Season 21 Alliance Tournament

January 1, 2021July 26, 2022

New this week in Mars Frontier:

End of Season 21 Alliance Tournament

🎇 Happy New Year, Martians! 🎇

From yesterday December 31st, 2020 – January 7th, 2021, rally your Alliance and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Alliance of Season 21!

Be one of the Top Alliances with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Alloy, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your Alliance.

Tournament ends on January 7th, 2020 at 1700 GMT at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season.

Season 22 Opens: January 7th, 2021

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “anti-alt” maps.

Game improvements

And that’s not all, Commander! We made some tweaks to the UI as well! Upgrade and unlock windows now support mouse-wheel scrolling. No more clicking on the arrows on the top left of those windows!
