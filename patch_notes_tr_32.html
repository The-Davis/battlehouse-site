TR: Elite Truck Bomb Update

December 9, 2017December 6, 2017

Updated this week in Thunder Run:

Elite Truck Bomb: The Turret Killer

patch_notes_tr

Several patches over the past few weeks have added more anti-turret power to the Elite Truck Bomb.

Here is a summary of the latest stats:

  • Large HP pool: up to 6,800 HP at max level
  • Extra DPS: the Elite Truck Bomb deals +50% extra damage against Turret Emplacements and other turret targets, over and above its listed DPS
  • PLUS, new this week: the Elite Truck Bomb now takes 70% less damage from turret weapon shots (up from 50% previously)

Have you given the Elite Truck Bomb a try yet? Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Landmine Balance Updates

December 8, 2017January 17, 2018

The Thunder Run design team has been working on updates to landmines of various types and levels. The goal of this work is to make each landmine upgrade useful and important, with a matching balance of power and cost.

As a first step, the following updates now take effect today:

Mine Production Balance

  • Production time of Anti-Air Landmines is now 10% faster at all levels.

Mine Defense Upgrades

On the defense side of things:

  • The Mine Defense stat on blueprint-based units can now be upgraded to Level 6, reducing damage taken from landmines by up to 21%.

More patches are likely in the future as the landmine design work continues.

Good hunting, Commanders!

    TR CPT Edison Levels, War Pirate Hardware, and Unit Improvements – November 23 Patch Notes

    November 23, 2017November 22, 2017

    New this week in Thunder Run:

    Double Hardware From War Pirate

    This week, War Pirate bases now feature double the amount of Hardware loot they had previously.

    TOC Leader Updates

    CPT Edison, the TOC Leader focused on Engineering, can now be upgraded to Level 48 in your Leader Barracks.

    • Level 48 specialization bonuses range up to 22.8% for research, repairs, or production, depending on which of the random specializations of CPT Edison you possess:
      • Research Engineer: Up to +22.8% to building upgrade speed and unit/turret research speed
      • Repair Engineer: Up to +22.8% to unit repair speed
      • Production Engineer: Up to +22.8% to unit training, turret mounting, and Yard collection speed

    To acquire CPT Edison, start by hiring LT Edison in the Store, and use your Leader Barracks to level him up. Once LT Edison reaches Level 21, he promotes to CPT and randomly gains one of the three specializations named above.

    Unit Improvements

    The Elite Truck Bomb now does +50% damage against turrets at all levels.

    Good hunting, commanders!
