TR: We Have a Patreon Now!

February 4, 2022February 4, 2022

New this week in Thunder Run:

Optional Monthly Subscriptions Are Here

You read that right, Commanders! We’re officially on Patreon now, and we’re offering new monthly subscription packages that come jam packed with some serious firepower.

Before I get into the fun stuff: Why are we starting a Patreon, you might ask?

Once a bustling global Facebook Game company that employed dozens of developers and artists, Battlehouse has since shrunk to a core original team, who all share an undying passion for making the most fun and immersive real time strategy game that we possibly can.

Over the years, as many popular Facebook and browser gamers made shifts to mobile, console, or traditional PC games, our valued community has kept up alive as one of the top Facebook Games on the Facebook Canvas platform, but support and opportunities for growth are beginning to wane as the popularity of alternate platforms increases.

Last year, we developed a desktop client for our flagship Facebook title: “Thunder Run: War of Clans”, that allowed us to rebrand Thunder Run as “Thunder Run: Global Warfare” and cross-publish to the Microsoft Games Store. We have big plans for getting on additional platforms like Steam and further expanding the World of Gundar for years to come, but we can’t do that without your help! 

Becoming an official Patron for Thunder Run: Global Warfare will help ensure that our small development team can continue making big necessary updates to the game every week. Just recently we launched a new Missile Silo building (with a full “craftable missiles” system), implemented Air Traffic Control towers (with a new tier of “aircraft” units), and we’re just getting started. There’s so much more we want to do!

Developing games is expensive, and we want to keep expanding Thunder Run and World of Gundar until we can all agree that it’s the best free to play multiplayer real time strategy gaming experience the market has to offer. Help us make it so!

Become an official Battlehouse Backer today!

Head on over to our new Patreon page to read more about the explosive subscription packages we have to offer: monthly gold supplies, Discord benefits, Thunder Run merch, the ability to take part in a “Voice of the Commander” focus group, and so much more!

TR: End of Season 24 Clan Tournament

December 30, 2021December 29, 2021

New this week in Thunder Run:

End-of-Season 24 Clan Tournament

Heads up, Commanders!

From December 30th – January 6th, 2021, rally your Clan and head to the Thunder Dome for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 24!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on January 6th, 2021 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 25 Opens: January 6th, 2021

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Dome” maps.
