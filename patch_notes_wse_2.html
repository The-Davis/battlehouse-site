WSE: Sanctuary Explained – September 22nd Patch Notes

September 22, 2022September 22, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Attention Martians!

In the beginning of September, we have rolled out an update to our Terms of Service and have mentioned it in the Patch Notes for that day.

Part of the update that was of most interest to a lot of players were the policies regarding alt accounts and account sharing. This update, and the warning of “DO NOT SHARE ACCOUNTS” has been foreshadowed as early as at least December of 2021, as per this post on Discord.

Our aim is to be simultaneously stricter on these policies, while also being more lenient. Stricter in a sense that we’re doing more aggressive account sharing detection and active-but-hidden alt detection; and more lenient, in a sense that low-count offenders are not necessarily moved to the Prison map right away. Hence, the introduction of “Sanctuary“.

Here are a few FAQs that we have gotten, and possible explanations.

What is Sanctuary?

Sanctuary is where a player base goes when it is involved in a violation of, typically, but not limited to, the alt-account policy.

What is the Difference Between Sanctuary and Prison?

Previously, offenders are sent to a different map or to Prison. If a player is in Prison, they will be unable to get out unless the imprisonment was a mistake, and an admin releases the player.

With Sanctuary, the player is free to move out on after a set number of days, depending on the violation. However, a player that makes frequent trips to Sanctuary will ultimately find themselves in Prison, and will no longer be able to get out.

Why am I in Sanctuary?

The player is sent an in-game message of why they were sent to Sanctuary, as well as for how long they will need to wait before they can go back to the map type they’ve been banished from. The length of the sentence depends on the violation incurred.

Effective today, if you are in Sanctuary, you will be able to jump to a map that you specifically have not been banished from.

For example, if you were moved to Sanctuary for an alt-violation, you will be banished from anti-alt maps for X days, but you are free to move to an alt-allowed map until your sentence is over.

As always, if you feel that you have been mis-identified as an alt, please contact support.

I received no message on why I am in Sanctuary/Prison

Some players have found themselves in Sanctuary or Prison without an in-game message like above. In most cases, there are likely other reasons why they have been relocated.

Please contact our support team to check what your account status is. Or alternatively, send a direct message to bh-wulfstan on Discord.

I am in Sanctuary, but cannot move anywhere

Please refer to “I received no message…” section above.

The Future of Sanctuary

We will continue to refine our Sanctuary and Prison systems and sentences, so this may change in the near future – with the name change from “Sanctuary” to something else maaaybe being in the nearer future. We will be making an announcement of any such change when it happens.

Have feedback to share about this week’s releases? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our Official Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

WSE: Auto-Migrate is Here!

August 11, 2022August 10, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Attention, Martians!

You can now auto-migrate your Facebook account details over to the desktop client! No more contacting support, no more jumping through hoops, nothing!

Interested? Please follow the steps below based on which scenario fits you.

Scenario 1: You have never tried our client before. Here are three easy steps to do it!

  1. Install client and run it.
  2. Select “Use Facebook” and log in with the account you use for the game
  3. Click Play Now.

Scenario 2: You have tried the client, logged in with Facebook, but have not migrated yet and want to do so. Please follow these steps:

  1. Install the client, and log in
  2. Click Account Info
  3. Scroll down and look for “Auto-Migrate Existing Account”. If it is Off, click on “Click to Enable”
  4. Click Play Now.

Scenario 3: You have tried the client before, but didn’t use the “Login with Facebook” option AND have not yet migrated, please use these steps:

  1. Check if you have a Facebook account associated with your login by going to https://www.battlehouse.com/bh_login/login/
  2. Click the button for the login method you currently use in the desktop client.
  3. Click on “Click here to manage your account.”
  4. If “Linked Facebook Login: (none) appears, copy down the email address that appears further up
  5. Go back to https://www.battlehouse.com/bh_login/login/
  6. Click “Continue with Facebook” and log in with the account you use for the game
  7. Click on “Click here to manage your account.”
  8. If the email address is the same as the one you already had, you’re done. If not, click on “Change” next to your email and enter the one you copied in step 4.
  9. Check your email and click the confirmation link.
  10. Follow the steps in Scenario 2

Note: Please follow the appropriate steps for your situation. As it was before, migrating accounts will result in loss of access of one of the accounts. Due to the technical challenge of unmerging accounts, we will not be able to do this for anyone in the foreseeable future.

Important: If you have already migrated your account via support or the migration tool, do NOT enable “Auto-Migrate Existing Account” on your client.

WSE: Desktop Client is Officially Available for Download!

July 23, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Attention, Martians!

The desktop client is now officially available for download! You can download it HERE.

Some of you may have gotten it in advance during the Facebook issue last week. If you did, no need to download again!

The difference this time is, you can migrate your main account to the desktop client – by yourself – and play from either platform! You can do this via the self-service migration tool, which can be found on the Store. Interested? Please follow the steps below VERY carefully.

Migration Steps

1. Download the client via the above link
2. Install the client, run, and create a new account, preferably, via Microsoft or Google.
3. Skip the tutorial, go to your player info, and write down your newbie account’s player ID.
4. Open up your main account on Facebook, and simultaneously run both for a bit so your newbie account is recognized as an alt.
5. Go to the Store and purchase the “Start Account Migration” item. It will not be active on the store unless you have your Battlehouse account recognized as an alt.

6. Activate item from Warehouse, and enter your newbie account’s player ID.

7. Go back to your newbie account on the client, and check the mail for the “Confirm Account Migration” item.

8. Activate the item from Warehouse, and restart the Thunder Run client. You should now see your main account. If you don’t, please wait 1-2 hours and run the client again.

If you want your old Facebook avatar in-game while using the downloadable client, please go here and log in with your client credentials, and change your avatar from there.

IMPORTANT: As you may have noticed from the screenshots, there are at least THREE warnings that you will LOSE the alt account. If you complete the whole process, we take it that you have acknowledged all three warnings and understood what would happen.

New features will be added to the desktop client over time, so stay tuned!

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our Official Discord Server.

WSE: War Star Empire Disabled by Facebook, Beta Client for Play – July 14th Patch Notes

July 14, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Attention, Commanders!

War Star Empire is currently disabled by Facebook as part of their regular policy compliance sweeps. All other Battlehouse games have passed this year’s review, and we’re not sure why War Star did not, considering that our compliance configurations are identical in all games.

We are currently working with Facebook to resolve this issue, and we appreciate your patience while we bring the game back to Facebook.

If you can’t wait for the Facebook issues to resolve to get back into the action, we can have an impromptu beta of the War Star desktop client. You can download it here: https://bit.ly/3IB7u7I

To use the app, please follow these steps.

  1. Install and open the app
  2. Create a new account (for quickest processing, we recommend you use your Facebook account for this)
  3. Start the game and find that you now have a “new” Level 1 account
  4. Grab the player ID for this “newbie” account (you may need to play a little bit of the tutorial first)
  5. Send this “newbie” player ID, and your original WSE player ID (or callsign) to:
    • Wulfstan (https://bit.ly/3uH16pJ) via direct message on Facebook
    • bh-nirgal on Discord (https://bit.ly/3c4knuG) via direct message (right-click on his name, then click “Message”)
    • Support team (https://bit.ly/3Piu8nm)

Step 5 is absolutely necessary, as we will need to migrate your Facebook account to the client. Without this step, you will NOT be able to play your old account on the beta client.

Friends Bar Clan Integration

And that’s not all, Commander!

For the past few months, the gifting system has been popping in and out of existence. Unfortunately, this is due to Facebook breaking something on their side, and is not something we are able to fix on ours.

So we’ve been looking for ways to break free of this reliance on Facebook, and the first step to do that is now here: the “Friends” bar will now show clan members!

A transparent clan logo on a player in your friend bar will indicate that they are a clanmate.

As mentioned before, this is just the beginning. This will be developed further down the road, so stay tuned for more updates!

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our Official Discord Server.

WSE: End of Season 21 Clan Tournament

June 30, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

End of Season 21 Clan Tournament

Heads up, Martians!

From June 30th – July 7th, 2022, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 21!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on July 7th, 2022 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 22 opens on July 7th, 2022

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Highlands” maps.

“Zombie” Battalion Debuff Update Details

And that’s not all, Commander! Last week, we released an update that was intended to address feedback regarding dead or “zombie” battalions. We’d like to clarify a few things, so here are some more details.

The weakened unit debuff previously applied only to attackers, as attackers often have an advantage over defenders due to having a human at the controls. This was causing some unintended effects in the field.

Base defenders and batts immediately next to their own home base do not get the debuff when defending. Batts deployed out in the field now get the debuff.

Keep your batts in fighting shape, Commander, and call them home when they get low on health.

Batt speed will likely soon be adjusted as well to take into account the speed debuff that applies to weakened units.
