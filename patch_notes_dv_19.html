DV: Welcome to Season 10! Elite Nimr Availability, Warehouse Upgrades, Elite AH-1 Cobra Unit Improvement – April 4th Patch Notes

April 4, 2019April 3, 2019

New this week in Days of Valor:

Welcome to Season 10!

Welcome to Season 10! Going into the new season, all Leaderboards and PvP Points are reset, meaning that it’s a fresh start for YOUR Clan to be the new the dominating Clan of Gundar, and win the Seasonal Clan Tournament Grand Prize of 10,000 Gold for your Clan at the end of the Season in 3 months from now. Does your Clan have what it takes?

Elite Nimr Availability

The grease monkeys of Gundar Arms Industries have finally received their backordered shipment of red paint meaning that the Elite Nimr is now available for mass production!

  • The Elite Nimr features enhanced stats over the Nimr, and can fire upwards! Choppers beware.
  • Starting Today: The Elite Nimr L6+ Blueprints are available again for all players in the ONP Store.

Get to the ONP Store now to start unlocking your Elite Nimr blueprints & sub-components.

Warehouse Upgrades

Following the release of the various new types of blueprints, unit equipment sets, strike teams, and strike squads now available: our Quatermaster has been in a quandary trying to figure out how he’s going to fit everything in storage. Fear not, Commanders! Upgrades to the Warehouse are here.

  • Warehouse hit points have been tripled at all levels with no changes to repair times.
  • Warehouse Levels 5 – 12 now contain two additional inventory slots.
  • Warehouse Level 13 now contains six additional inventory slots.
  • Warehouse Levels 2 – 12 now cost less supply and fuel to construct.
  • Warehouse Levels 5 – 12 will take longer to build than before (L5 now takes as long as the previous L6, L6 takes as long as the previous L7, all the way up to L12). This change has no effect on the Level 13 Warehouse.

We have been observing all of the Community’s great feedback, and we hear you loud and clear! As such, we’re considering several additional Warehouse improvements to make unlocking more inventory space easier, while granting more control over item sorting. While we don’t yet have a date set for the next batch of Warehouse upgrades, more information will be coming soon.

Elite AH-1 Cobra Unit Improvement (AIM-9 Sidewinder Weaponry)

Don’t worry, the grease monkeys didn’t forget about this week’s unit improvements. Try out the updated and upgraded Elite AH-1 Cobra which is now packing some serious air-to-air heat with it’s Aim-9 Sidewinder weaponry. No additional purchase or upgrade required! If you already have the Elite AH-1 Cobra unlocked, you already have the new Aim-9 Sidewinder weaponry.

  • The new Aim-9 Sidewinder weaponry grants the Elite AH-1 a new special attack that’s bound to the “E” key.
  • Press “E” on your keyboard while in battle to launch a targeted missile strike. The missiles take a little while to reload, but deal mega-damage to enemy choppers.

Have feedback to share about this week’s releases? Let us know over on Clan HQ.

Good hunting, Commanders!

DV: OH-58 Kiowa Strike Squadrons – March 21st Patch Notes

March 21, 2019March 21, 2019

New this week in Days of Valor:

OH-58 Kiowa Strike Squadrons

Commander, we can now place alert beacons on some of our Ambush Points to call in our Kiowa Strike Squadrons. These reserve OH-58s will give the enemy a nasty surprise. Our surveyors have found room for a few more Ambush Points to accommodate our new reserves.

  • Ambush Point limit raised by two. You will need to research the next Ambush Point quantity tech to unlock the extras spots.
  • Deploy up to two OH-58 Kiowa Strike Squadrons to your Ambush Points.

OH-58 Kiowa Strike Squadrons can be upgraded in your Weapons Lab. At Level 1, three OH-58s deploy when the Ambush Point is triggered. You can currently research up to Level 5, which will result in seven OH-58s deploying when the Ambush Point is triggered.

Even if the enemy retreats, the deployed OH-58s will remain until destroyed or you return to repair your base. Don’t forget, Strike Team and Strike Squadron units benefit from equips and unit levels.

Head to the Menace Event ONP Prize store to purchase your OH-58 Kiowa Strike Squadron blueprints, and 2 OH-58 Kiowa Strike Squad units today!

UH-60 Blackhawk Airdrop Troops

Also available now in the Menace Event ONP Prize store:

Collect all new Airdrop Troop sets for all of your favorite infantry units. In addition to the Riflemen and Machine Gunners that are already available, the Mortarman, Javelin Gunner, Sniper, Sapper, Combat Engineer, and Stinger Gunner are joining the fray. Collect ’em all so that your enemy will never know what’s coming!

Good hunting, Commanders!

Changes to Infantry & Ground Units On Water Terrain

Following the release of the UH-60 Blackhawk a couple weeks ago, we’ve been hearing reports of air cavalry getting stuck on water, and some (like the infamous “Stinger Jesus” pictured above) have even been walking on water to pursue their attackers. It’s a miracle I tell you!

Time to put the miracles to rest, however – at least for now. As of today, all infantry placed on water tiles will be subject to crossing paths with the fearsome man eating sharks of Gundar’s oceans (in the case of infantry), or will sink (in the case of armor vehicles).

Careful around those shores, Commander! And good hunting!

DV: New UH-60 Blackhawk Unit, Roadblock Company Strike Teams, L2 Unit Equips, and more! – February 7th Patch Notes

February 7, 2019February 7, 2019

New this week in Days of Valor:

New UH-60 Blackhawk Unit

Air Cavalry, mount up! The UH-60 Blackhawk is ready for deployment, Commander. This tough troop transport is a capable anti-infantry gunship in its own right, but it can also deploy a variety of ground troops to smash through whatever defensive strategy the enemy deploys. The troop compartment does need repairs between deployments, but our grease monkeys assure us that they can handle it. Obtain all the necessary sub-component blueprints before the ONP Event ends. But beware. When they’re gone, they’re gone.

  • Press the ‘E’ key to deploy your troops at the time and place of your choosing. They’ll still deploy if the Blackhawk is shot down, but will suffer a 50% hit point penalty.
  • All UH-60 Blackhawks deploy Riflemen, and deploy Elite Riflemen at level 15.
  • Buy air-dropped troops to supplement the UH-60’s payload. An equip with four Machine Gunners is given for free when you first unlock the unit.
  • Unlock Level 6 before the event week ends, or be capped at Level 5! The blueprint and sub-component are cheaper to help out with this.
  • You will have enough time to unlock all five levels before the event ends, so long as you crush your enemies and obtain ONP quickly.

Be wary, Commander! Intel reports that the enemy is already fielding UH-60 Blackhawks at several of their bases!

Roadblock Company Strike Teams

Roadblock Company is ready to roll out, Commander. These boys delight in making sure nothing else can roll out. Three squads are ready to deploy to your Ambush Points. You can now upgrade your Ambush Point to allow you to field Vanguard Company and Roadblock Company at the same time.

  • Able Squad fields six Machine Gunners and five Panzerschrecks, plus a sixth Panzerschreck if you field the entire company.
  • Bravo Squad’s spotters field six Panzerschrecks and five Mortarmen, plus an MQ-8B spotter drone if you field the entire company.
  • Charlie Squad’s logistics team fields eight Panzerschrecks and three Riflemen, plus a GPW if you field the entire company.

Don’t forget, your Strike Teams get stronger as you upgrade their component units. Be sure to take advantage of the recent level 13 unit blueprints in the Hardware Store for a considerable strength boost.

New Level 2 Unit Equipment

Gundar Arms Industries has officially unveiled new weapons technologies for several popular units, and 3 new sets are now available by purchasing Level 1 sets in the ONP Store and combining parts to make Level 2 sets!

Set #1 – Adler Kfz:

  • Adler Kfz Laser Sights II – Improved Laser Sight technology that increases range by 5%
  • Adler Kfz Ballistic Armor MK II – Improved armor coating that reduces damage taken by 10%
  • Adler Kfz AP Ammunition II – The newest grade armor-piercing ammunition that increases damage by 10%

Set #2 – Mi-24D Hind:

  • Mi-24D Hind Laser Sights II – Laser homing technology that increases range by 5%
  • Mi-24D Hind Ballistic Armor MK II – Improved armor coating reduces damage taken by 10%
  • Mi-24D Hind AP Ammunition II – The newest grade armor-piercing ammunition that increases damage by 10%

Set #3 – OH-58 Kiowa:

  • OH-58 Kiowa Laser Sights II – Laser homing technology that increases range by 5%
  • OH-58 Kiowa Ballistic Armor MK II – Improved armor coating reduces damage taken by 10%
  • OH-58 Kiowa AP Ammunition II – The newest grade armor-piercing ammunition that increases damage by 10%

Check your Weapons Factory -> Equipment menu to get started upgrading unit equipment to L2. Just like L2 leaders, this process requires obtaining a second copy of the L1 item to perform the upgrade. L1 unit equipment is available as usual in the Store -> Event Prizes -> Unit Equipment menu.
