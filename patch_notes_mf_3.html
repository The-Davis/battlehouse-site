MF: End of Season 26 Alliance Tournament

March 31, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in Mars Frontier:

End of Season 26 Alliance Tournament

From March 31st, 2022 – April 7th, 2022, rally your Alliance and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Alliance of Season 26!

Be one of the Top Alliances with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Alloy, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your Alliance.

Tournament ends on April 7th, 2022 at 1700 GMT at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season.

Season 27 Opens: April 7th, 2022

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Lowlands” maps.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “mars-frontier” channel within our Official Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!
