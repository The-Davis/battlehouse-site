TR: “Shark Tank” Level Update for Season 8

October 3, 2017October 31, 2017

Coming in Thunder Run Season 8 on October 5th:

PvP Level Range Update

In the past season, as soon as players reached Level 39, they could be attacked by anyone, including L50+ attackers. With the increasing power of newly-released elite units, it has become clear that players in the high-30s/low-40s level range still need a few more levels to upgrade their forces before becoming “fair game” for maxed-out attackers.

Starting with Season 8, which begins October 5 at 1700GMT, the new PvP level limits will be as follows, for attacks on home bases:

  • Level 39 and below: attack anyone 4 levels below you, up to 4 levels above you
  • Level 40+: see chart below

These limits apply only to home bases. Battalions and quarries can always be attacked regardless of the level difference.

Elite Stryker

The Elite Stryker has undergone DVH and ECP upgrade programs, adding thicker Double V-Hull protective armor and a new 450-horsepower engine for greater mobility. Plus, stronger surface-to-surface firepower rounds it out as a superior light armored vehicle.
