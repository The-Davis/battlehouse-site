New this week in Thunder Run:

Level 13 AH-1 Cobra

The Level 13 AH-1 Cobra is here!

As with the recent unit level cap releases, your Level 13 AH-1 Cobra will also feature improved stats and no increases in battalion space requirements. As with any improvement in power, there are increases in cost, production time, and repair time.

TR: Regional Map Entry Requirements Update

April 12, 2019

Today in Thunder Run:

Regional Map Entry Requirements Update

In an effort to better teach incoming new players about the regional map, and welcome them to the map sooner in their Thunder Run journeys: New players can now access the regional map and deploy battalions as early as TOC L2, by upgrading the Reserves Bay to L2.

Please note that effective today, Reserves Bay L3 or higher is now required in order to deploy a full 10 battalions to the map. Commanders with an L2 Reserves Bay will only be able to deploy a maximum of 2 battalions to the map until they’re able to upgrade to L3.

Thunder Run Veterans, rest assured: these changes will have no effect on Commanders who already have a Reserves Bay L3 or higher.

Good hunting, Commanders!

MF: Opportunity Hero Unit – April 11th Patch Notes

April 11, 2019July 26, 2022

New this week in Mars Frontier:

Opportunity Hero Unit

To celebrate the recent real-life retirement of NASA’s hardest-working robot in the solar system, The Opportunity Mars Rover, Mars Frontier brings to you a very special treat in the form of the all new Opportunity Hero Unit available now!

The Opportunity Hero Unit is a special unit you can install as a guard to your Central Computer. If enemies approach the Central Computer, the laser-armed Opportunity rolls out to blast your rivals to smithereens.

Obtain your Opportunity Hero Unit in this week’s Kalamity Event by spending Frazzle Chips in the Event Prizes Store. Store the Opportunity Hero in your warehouse, and then when ready, equip it to your Central Computer via the Central Computer upgrade menu.

Good hunting, Commanders!
