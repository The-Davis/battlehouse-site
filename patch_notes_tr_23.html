TR: New Elite Nimr Unit – December 27th Patch Notes

December 27, 2018January 4, 2019

New this week in Thunder Run:

Elite Nimr Blueprints

The grease monkeys have been going bananas, Commander. They tweaked some of our Nimr rigs and gave them a shiny new coat of red paint. Not only do you have the desert optimization and anti-armor missiles you’ve grown to love, but we also can knock choppers out of the sky. The mechanics wanted to challenge our troops. It isn’t enough to just obtain the blueprint. There are sub-components you can use to soup up your ride. But beware. When they’re gone, they’re gone.

  • The Elite Nimr features enhanced stats over the Nimr, and can fire upwards! Choppers beware.
  • Unlock Level 6 before the event week ends, or be capped at Level 5! The blueprint and sub-component are cheaper to help out with this.
  • You will have enough time to unlock all five levels before the event ends, so long as you crush your enemies and obtain ONP quickly.

If you miss out on the unlock, never fear. We will be fully unlocking the Elite Nimr later next year for players who don’t complete the upgrades on time. Your technological edge will only last so long, so you’d better take advantage of it!

Get to the ONP Store now to start unlocking your Elite Nimr blueprints & sub-components. After your Elite Nimr hits L5, make sure you return to the ONP store to buy the (free) L6 Blueprint before the ONP Event ends on January 3rd, 2019.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Landmine Improvements – December 13th Patch Notes

December 13, 2018December 12, 2018

New this week in Thunder Run:

Landmine Improvements

You’d think digging a hole in the ground and tossing in a bomb was an easy task, but linking our mines to our command and control systems has always proven a little difficult. Fortunately, our engineers have managed to squeeze more minefields into our network, and have improved the power of our anti heavy-armor mines. Shall I give the order to start building?

Updates Include:

  • Minefield limit raised by 2.
  • Anti Heavy Armor Mine level cap raised to 10.

If you already have Level 5 Anti Heavy Armor Mines, you can start upgrading to Level 6 right away. Level 7 requires a Level 7 Weapons Lab. The new minefield size limit requires a Level 14 Weapon Factory.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Turret Limit Increase – November 29th Patch Notes

November 29, 2018November 29, 2018

New this week in Thunder Run:

Turret Limit Increase

I hope you participated in the land grab, Commander, because our architects report that they are ready to deploy more turrets than before. Naturally, we’ll be boosting our generator capacity too to keep up with demand.

Updates Include:

  • Max number of Turret Emplacements increased by 4.
  • Turret head type limits increased by 1 for each type (applies to blueprint-based turret heads and the TOW)
  • Generator max level is raised to L19 to support the power requirements for extra turrets

To take advantage of these new limits, upgrade your TOC to level 11 and start building!
