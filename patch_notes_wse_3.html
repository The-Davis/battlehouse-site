WSE: Lightning Cannons are Online – April 21st Patch Notes

April 21, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

Lightning Cannon

The Lightning Cannon is here, and is now available for deployment to your Turret Platforms. Lightning Cannons fire disruptive energy beams at enemy aircraft and projectile weapons like missiles. To unlock it, visit the “Weapon Blueprints” section of the Event Prizes Store to obtain the new blueprints, and then unlock the new tech in your Weapons Lab.

WSE: End of Season 20 Clan Tournament

March 31, 2022July 26, 2022

New this week in War Star Empire:

End of Season 20 Clan Tournament

Heads up, Martians!

From March 31st, 2022 – April 7th, 2022, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 20!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on April 7th, 2022 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 21 opens on April 7th, 2022

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Highlands” maps.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over in the “war-star-empire” channel within our Official Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!
