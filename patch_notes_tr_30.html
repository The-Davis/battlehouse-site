New Anti-Tank Mine Levels and Balance Update – February 15 Patch Notes

February 15, 2018February 15, 2018

Following on the previous improvements to anti-infantry mines in Thunder Run and Days of Valor, the following new changes take effect today:

Anti-Armor Mine Updates

  • Anti-Armor mines can now be upgraded to Level 19 for increased damage and splash range.
    • L19 mines are capable of destroying any BRDM-3 or low-level M1 Abrams tanks in one hit!
  • Production of Anti-Armor mines is now 20-30% faster at all levels.
  • However, to balance the faster production time, Anti-Armor mines now do 20-30% less damage when they explode.

More patches are likely in the future as the landmine design work continues.

Good hunting, Commanders!

New Levels for CPT Savage – February 2 Patch Notes

February 2, 2018January 31, 2018

New this week in Thunder Run and Days of Valor:

TOC Leader Updates

CPT Savage, the TOC Leader focused on battle tactics, can now be upgraded to Level 48 in your Leader Barracks.

  • Level 48 specialization bonuses range up to 22.8% for your units or turret weapons, depending on which of the random specializations of CPT Savage you possess:
    • Offense Tactics: Up to +22.8% to unit weapon DPS and damage resistance
    • Defense Tactics: Up to +22.8% to turret weapon DPS and damage resistance

To acquire CPT Savage, start by hiring LT Savage in the Store, and use your Leader Barracks to level him up. Once LT Savage reaches Level 21, he promotes to CPT and randomly gains one of the two specializations named above.

Good hunting, commanders!

TR: New Blast Shield Technology – February 1 Patch Notes

February 1, 2018January 31, 2018

New this week in Thunder Run:

Blast Defense Upgrades

All blueprint-based units now feature an upgradable Blast Shield that reduces damage from most splash weapons, including Mortar Emplacements and units like the TOS-1A.

To get started, open the Research menu for any blueprint-based unit, and upgrade the Blast Shield stat.

This new research requires Hardware and maxes out at 7% damage reduction (with more levels planned for the future).

To see exactly which weapons this new Blast Shield protects you against, check for the “Blast” icon under “Weapon Type” on unit and turret Upgrade menus.

This icon means that the unit or turret deals “Blast” damage and is therefore affected by your upgraded Blast Shields.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Landmine Balance Updates – January 18 Patch Note

January 18, 2018January 17, 2018

The Thunder Run design team has been working on updates to landmines of various types and levels. The goal of this work is to make each landmine upgrade useful and important, with a matching balance of power and cost.

Following the previous reduction in repair time for anti-air mines, the following new updates take effect today:

Mine Power Balance

  • Production of Anti-Infantry Landmines is now 30% faster at all levels.
  • However, Anti-Infantry Landmines now do 30% less damage.
