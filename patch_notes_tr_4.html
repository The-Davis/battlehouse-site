TR: Welcome to Season 26!

April 14, 2022April 13, 2022

New this week in Thunder Run:

Welcome to Season 26!

Welcome to Season 26! Going into the new season, all Leaderboards and PvP Points are reset, meaning that it’s a fresh start for YOUR Clan to be the new the dominating Clan of Gundar, and win the Seasonal Clan Tournament Grand Prize of 10,000 Gold for your Clan at the end of the Season in 3 months from now. Does your Clan have what it takes?

New Batch of Elite Unit Skins

Heads up, Commander! The next batch of elite unit skins are here!

The Elite Stinger Gunner, Elite M1 Abrams, and the Elite Harbin Z-9W get their skins this week!

To get them, simply head on over to the Store! You can either go to Store>Cosmetics to purchase them with gold; or go to “Event Prizes” to purchase them with ONP/Hardware.

Friendly reminder that only “basic” skins can be purchased with ONP/Hardware. Premium skins, such as black (and more to come in the future) can only be bought with gold. And of course, red and green skins are free!

New “Edit Scenery UI” Component

And that’s not all, Commanders! We’ve added new button that allows you to interact with placed scenery items on your base. You can find it at the right side of the screen, just below the “Zoom” buttons.

Clicking the icon switches you from “Edit Scenery” to “Editing Scenery” and back.

You can only interact with scenery items when it is on the “Editing Scenery” state. You can also manage your base in this state, but may find moving troops around difficult. So please make sure the icon is on “Edit Scenery” state when you are not fiddling around with scenery items.

Looking for the Season 25 Tournament winners list? Don’t worry. It will be revealed soon. Stay tuned for another Patch Notes coming later on!

We have some exciting news, announcements, and more waiting for you on our most recent edition of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over on our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: End of Season 25 Clan Tournament and more!

April 7, 2022April 6, 2022

New this week in Thunder Run:

End-of-Season 25 Clan Tournament

Heads up, Commanders!

From April 7th – April 14th, 2022, rally your Clan and head to the Thunder Dome for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 25!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on April 14th, 2022 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 26 Opens: April 14th, 2022

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Dome” maps.

New Batch of Unit Skins!

The Sapper, Elite M109A7, and Elite OH-58F get their skins this week!

To get them, simply head on over to the Store! You can either go to Store>Cosmetics to purchase them with gold; or go to “Event Prizes” to purchase them with ONP/Hardware.

Friendly reminder that only “basic” skins can be purchased with ONP/Hardware. Premium skins, such as black (and more to come in the future) can only be bought with gold. And of course, red and green skins are free!

Once bought, just activate the skins and head on over to your Barracks, Motor Pool, or Airfield, and find the new option to change the unit colors.

Pick out the skin you bought, click “Apply Paint“, and you’re done!

More elite unit skins will be coming in the next few weeks, Commander. Care to guess which ones are next?

Place-able Scenery is Here!

And that’s not all, Commanders! Placeable scenery is here!

Scenery blueprints are available for purchase using ONP, Hardware, or gold. Just head on over to the Store and go to any Cosmetic menu to find the Scenery blueprints!

After purchasing your blueprint of choice, simply go to Buildings>Scenery to place them anywhere in your base!

Migration Lottery Update

First of all, congratulations to all the lottery winners of the last few weeks! Accounts have been migrated, and they can now choose to play thru the desktop client.

We’re going to take a small break from running the weekly lottery, and perhaps in the future consider running them monthly instead of weekly. Fret not! We still have one more post up that we have yet to draw a winner from!

Game improvements

And that’s not all, Commanders! We have made a few game improvements based on player feedback!

  • Regional map battalion icons will now display your custom unit skin, if applicable. They will also be displayed in other areas, such as the “Train” and the “Unlock & Upgrade” menus.
  • Minor change: when applying a skin, the window will now close and show the coloring progress bar.
  • Unit displayed on battalion is now the one with the highest space requirement that at least has 25% HP. So if a battalion of one rifleman and dead Elite TOS-1A will now show the rifleman until the TOS-1A is healed up to at least 25% of its HP.
