TR: End of Season 20 Clan Tournament

January 1, 2021January 1, 2021

This week in Thunder Run:

End-of-Season 20 Clan Tournament

🎇 Happy New Year, Commanders! 🎇

From yesterday December 31st, 2020 – January 7th, 2021, rally your Clan and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 20!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on January 7th, 2021 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 21 Opens: January 7th, 2021

  • NEW: Welcome to the Thunder Dome – Starting next season, January 7, only the new “Thunder Dome” map (TOC6+ required) will be ladder-enabled. Get ready to rumble in the dome!
  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “anti-alt” maps.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over on our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Voice of the Commander Updates, BMP-1 and Elite BMP-1 updates – December 31st Patch Notes

December 31, 2020December 31, 2020

This weekend in Thunder Run:

Voice of the Commander Updates

We’re celebrating with a quality-of-life update based on your player feedback that’s intended to address a few of the issues and suggestions that you’ve brought forth to us via the “Voice of the Commander” trackers. Don’t see the update you were hoping for in this week’s release? Fear not, as there is still plenty more to come.

Deactivate your mines

We have heard your feedback loud and clear, and now, it is possible to disable minefields.

You can now disable your minefields during single-player AI attacks if you wish. This will automatically remove itself if you log out, or can be removed any time you want by clicking the status indicator icon and choosing “Remove” or clicking “Manage” on any mine and then clicking “Enable Minefields”

Known issue: When building new mines, the “Disable/Enable mine” button overlaps with the progress bar. This will be fixed really soon.

BMP-1 and Elite BMP-1 Updates

In preparation for customizable loadouts that we’ll be rolling out in the near future, we’ve made small changes to the BMP-1 and Elite BMP-1 units.

Particularly, the BMP-1 will have its special troops default back to stock Riflemen, and the Elite BMP-1 units are defaulted to Elite Riflemen. The Elite BMP-1 has also been given a slight damage increase.

Some may see this as a nerf, but don’t fret, commander! Customizable loadouts are coming and it’ll be worth it!

Upgrade/Unlock mouse-wheel support

And that’s not all, Commander! We made some tweaks to the UI as well! Upgrade and unlock windows now support mouse-wheel scrolling. No more clicking on the arrows on the top left of those windows!

To see where the design team currently stands on various Community suggested features and game improvements, check out the Thunder Run Voice of the Commander Tracker.

We have some exciting news, announcements, and more waiting for you on our most recent edition of the Battlehouse Dev Blog.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over on our Thunder Run Discord Server.

🎄 Happy Holidays, Commanders! 🎄
