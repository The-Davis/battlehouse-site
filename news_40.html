MF: Welcome to Season 21!

October 8, 2020July 26, 2022

New this week in Mars Frontier:

Welcome to Season 21!

Welcome to Season 21! Going into the new season, all Leaderboards and PvP Points are reset, meaning that it’s a fresh start for YOUR Alliance to be the new the dominating Alliance of the Season, and win the Seasonal Clan Tournament Grand Prize of 10,000 Gold for your Alliance at the end of the Season in 3 months from now. Does your Alliance have what it takes?

Curiosity Unit upgrades

And that’s not all, Commander! Our engineers just had a breakthrough on the Curiosity, and it can now be made stronger than ever. The Curiosity level cap has been increased from Level 8 to 13!

Looking for the Season 20 Tournament winners list? Don’t worry. It will be revealed soon. Stay tuned for another Patch Notes coming later today!

FS: Tactical Ops Center Level 17 – October 1st Patch Notes

October 1, 2020October 1, 2020

New this week in Firestrike:

Tactical Ops Center Level 17

Your architects have been hard at work, Commander. In addition to a sharply-worded requisition order for more coffee, they’ve delivered the upgrade plans for the next Tactical Ops Center expansion. It seems like only yesterday we were huddled together in tents. 

Effective today:

The Tactical Ops Center level cap is raised to Level 17, paving the way for new maximum levels for all buildings, turrets, landmines, and top-tier units.

New units

And that’s not all, Commander! Reinforcements have arrived. Today, the Commando, the TOS-1M, and the UH-60 Blackhawk will take the field.

MF: End of Season 20 Alliance Tournament – October 1st Patch Notes

October 1, 2020July 26, 2022

New this week in Mars Frontier:

End of Season 20 Alliance Tournament

From October 1st – October 8th, 2020, rally your Alliance and head to the regional map for your chance to be the strongest Alliance of Season 19!

Be one of the Top Alliances with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Alloy, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your Alliance.

Tournament ends on October 8th, 2020 at 1700 GMT at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season.

Season 21 Opens: October 8th, 2020

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “anti-alt” maps.

TR: Hardware Insta-build Event! – September 18th Patch Notes

September 18, 2020September 17, 2020

New this week in Thunder Run:

Hardware Insta-build Event!

Hey, Commanders! This weekend is the perfect time to visit the Hardware Store!

Through the weekend of September 18th to September 21st, 2020, a new Global Aura will be in effect that allows instant-build and instant-research for hardware recipes.

What does this mean? Well, normally players would first have to spend the hardware and all needed resources to start the upgrade the slow way, and ‘then’ speed it up if they wanted to instant build it.

During this event, players have the option to “Insta Build w/ Gold” any upgrade or research that requires Hardware.

And that’s not all, Commander! All players will be able to buy Hardware recipes throughout the weekend event period.

DV: New Elite Panzerschreck, Hardware Insta-build Event and more! – September 17th Patch Notes

September 17, 2020September 17, 2020

New this week in Days of Valor:

Elite Panzerschreck

Target locked, Commanders! Rockets away!

Amongst all the new training regimens recently made available to several of our top Infantry in the form of expanded level caps, an all new unit class has emerged, and is available now. Say hello to the Elite Panzerschreck!

Features Include:

  • The Elite Panzerschreck has improved range, firepower, damage over time, and has higher HP than the Javelin Gunner.
  • “Ballistic shield” ability: Press E to make the Elite Panzerschreck practically invincible for a few seconds, at the cost of their move speed dropping to 25%.

Insta-build Event!

That’s not all, Commanders! Through the weekend of September 18th to September 21st, 2020, a new Global Aura will be in effect that allows instant-build and instant-research for hardware recipes. Keep an eye out for more details tomorrow!
