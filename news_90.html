DoV: Elite Truck Bomb Update

December 9, 2017December 6, 2017

Updated this week in Days of Valor:

Elite Truck Bomb: The Turret Killer

Several patches over the past few weeks have added more anti-turret power to the Elite Truck Bomb.

Here is a summary of the latest stats:

  • Large HP pool: up to 6,800 HP at max level
  • Damage resistance: ignores 70% of the damage from turret weapon shots
  • PLUS, new this week – Extra DPS: the Elite Truck Bomb deals an extra +100% damage against Turret Emplacements and other turret targets, over and above its listed DPS (up from +50% previously)

Have you given the Elite Truck Bomb a try yet? Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Landmine Balance Updates

December 8, 2017January 17, 2018

The Thunder Run design team has been working on updates to landmines of various types and levels. The goal of this work is to make each landmine upgrade useful and important, with a matching balance of power and cost.

As a first step, the following updates now take effect today:

Mine Production Balance

  • Production time of Anti-Air Landmines is now 10% faster at all levels.

Mine Defense Upgrades

On the defense side of things:

  • The Mine Defense stat on blueprint-based units can now be upgraded to Level 6, reducing damage taken from landmines by up to 21%.

More patches are likely in the future as the landmine design work continues.

Good hunting, Commanders!

DV: New Weapons Lab and Weapons Factory Upgrades

December 2, 2017November 28, 2017

New this week in Days of Valor:

Unlock and Build Turrets and Mines Faster

The Weapons Lab now features upgrades on the Research Rate stat, and the Weapons Factory now features upgrades on the Production Rate stat.

These upgrades lower the time needed to unlock and produce turret weapons and landmines by up to 4%, for a modest investment in ONP.

Check your building upgrade menus for details!
