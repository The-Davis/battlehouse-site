New this week in Thunder Run:

Mysterious unit spotted!

Heads up, Commander!

Scouts have caught sight of a new unit under Lansana’s command. Pilots and marksmen are usually their preferred company, and it’s been observed that they carry little to no firepower themselves. Optical gear, instead, has been glimpsed outside their tents. Trainers for these units among the ranks have also been distinguished. Target acquisition, our scouts have noticed, is the specialty of these trainers. Expect the unexpected during this round of Nomad event, Commander, while we attempt to further identify these new infantry units. Recruiting them over to our side may even be possible in the future; once they recognize we’re fighting for a good cause.

Welcome to Season 23!

Welcome to Season 23! TR: End of Season 22 Clan Tournament, Alt-Account Limit Update, and more – July 15th Patch Notes

July 15, 2021July 14, 2021

This week in Thunder Run:

End-of-Season 22 Clan Tournament

Heads up, Commanders!

From July 15th – July 22nd, 2021, rally your Clan and head to the Thunder Dome for your chance to be the strongest Clan of Season 22!

Be one of the Top Clans with the most Clan PvP Points to win between 3,000-10,000 Gold, divided evenly among the top 10 members of your clan.

Tournament ends on July 22nd, 2021 at 1700 GMT, at which point winners will be announced, prizes awarded, and leaderboards will be reset for the new season. It’s important to note that you should stay in the same Clan through the end of the tournament and for at least one hour afterward, until at least 1800 GMT to ensure your Clan’s prizes are scored correctly.

Season 23 Opens: July 22, 2021

  • The new season will begin immediately after the end of the tournament.
  • Please be reminded that PvP points can only be accumulated on “Dome” maps.

Alt-Cap Update in Alt-Allowed maps

Back in June 10th, we imposed a limit of 10 accounts in some pro-alt maps, with the intention of reducing it further.

Starting July 10th, maps that previously had a limit of 10 accounts will now have a limit of five accounts. This affects maps 215, 222, and 228.

Call Sign Reset

Starting July 15th, call signs with disallowed characters will no longer be allowed. Players with “invalid” call signs have been sent messages as far back as June 15th. Some may have asked for their call signs changed via support or Discord; but for those who did not get it changed, they will be given a free call sign change.

Ranged debuff to damaged units

And that’s not all, Commanders! We’ve received a lot of community feedback about damaged units, and have previously applied speed and damage reduction to them. From here on, these weak “zombie” units will also suffer range reduction.

Have new feedback to share? Let us know over on our Thunder Run Discord Server.

Good hunting, Commanders!

TR: Missile Upgrades and more – July 8th Patch Notes

July 8, 2021July 7, 2021

New this week in Thunder Run:

Missile Upgrades

Attention, Commanders!

Our engineers just had a breakthrough on our missile technology, and some of them now deliver a little extra kick!

The EMP Missile level cap has been increased to Level 16, the EMP Missile Volley level cap has been increased to Level 15, the High Explosive Missile level cap has been increased to Level 14, and the High Explosive Missile Volley level cap has been increased to Level 13!

Game improvements

And that’s not all, Commanders! When you log in to an attacked base, you will now be given a prompt to rearm all your mines.

The “Rearm Mines” button will direct you to the store buy more resources if you don’t have enough to craft any of the mines. Otherwise, it will batch the crafting commands for you.

We know, we know. It’s another pop-up window. If you prefer to rearm your mines manually, you can turn this off in your player preferences.

TR: AI Missile Defense Nerfs, and more – June 24th Patch Notes

June 24, 2021June 23, 2021

New this week in Thunder Run:

AI Missile Defense Nerfs

Attention, Commanders!

We have reviewed AI battle data, and last week, have made some tweaks to the missile defenses of AI bases.

Our intent has always been for players to use a combination of missiles and units to conquer AI bases; not to spam missiles and win, and not to have missiles be useless. Recently, the latter appears more true.

The strongest AI missile defenses will now have 24% chance to shoot down a missile (down from 48%). More importantly, the number of buildings with missile defenses will be reduced to 1/4 of the turrets on TOC levels 1-5, and 1/3 of turrets on TOC levels 6-12.

Missile upgrades

And that’s not all, Commanders!

Our engineers just had a breakthrough on our missile technology, and some of them now deliver a little extra kick!

The EMP Missile level cap has been increased to Level 15, the EMP Missile Volley level cap has been increased to Level 14, and the High Explosive Missile level cap has been increased to Level 13!
