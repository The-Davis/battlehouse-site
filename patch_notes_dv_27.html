DoV: New Levels for CPT Edison, More Unit Improvements – November 9 Patch Notes

November 9, 2017November 8, 2017

New this week in Days of Valor:

Unit Improvements

  • Elite Rifleman: 30% more HP at all levels. Now sports up to 8,750 HP at max level!
  • Elite Truck Bomb: Now takes 50% less damage from turrets, improved from 25% previously. Bombs away!

TOC Leader Updates

CPT Edison, the TOC Leader focused on Engineering, can now be upgraded to Level 48 in your Leader Barracks.

  • Level 48 specialization bonuses range up to 22.8% for research, repairs, or production, depending on which of the random specializations of CPT Edison you possess:
    • Research Engineer: Up to +22.8% to building upgrade speed and unit/turret research speed
    • Repair Engineer: Up to +22.8% to unit repair speed
    • Production Engineer: Up to +22.8% to unit training, turret mounting, and Yard collection speed

To acquire CPT Edison, start by hiring LT Edison in the Store, and use your Leader Barracks to level him up. Once LT Edison reaches Level 21, he promotes to CPT and randomly gains one of the three specializations named above.

Good hunting, Commanders!

Coming Soon: New “Revenge Rule” Following “Shark Tank” Changes

October 20, 2017October 20, 2017

The Community has spoken, and we hear you loud and clear! Coming October 23rd in Thunder Run and Days of Valor:

PvP Updates

Starting on October 23rd

  • The current “revenge” rule that activates when you launch an attack against a player’s home base will now also activate when you launch an attack against a player’s battalion. Landmine Production Bonuses, Turret Sale, and More! – October 19 Patch Notes

October 19, 2017October 19, 2017

New this week in Thunder Run and Days of Valor:

Building Improvements

The Level 11+ Weapons Factory now gives greater bonuses to production speed of landmines and turret weapons. The speed bonus is now +85% for a Level 14 Weapons Factory.

The Level 9+ Leader Barracks now gives a greater bonus to leader training speed. The bonus is now +210% for a Level 10 Leader Barracks.

Unit Updates

Several blueprint-based units are increasing in toughness. The following units now have 20-30% greater HP at all levels:

  • BRDM-3
  • Elite OH-58
  • Elite Rifleman
  • Truck Bomb
  • MQ-8B

Security teams will no longer activate from units that begin a battle with less than 50% HP. To deploy a security team when a unit is destroyed, the unit must start the battle with at least 50% HP.

Event Prizes Turret Blueprint Sale

This week only – the Event Prizes store includes big ONP discounts on defensive turret blueprints:

Level 11-12 blueprints:

  • FASCAM
  • M45 Quadmount
  • C-RAM
  • HEL Laser
  • Artillery

Level 11-12 and Level 13-14 blueprints:

  • MG Tower
  • Mortar
  • TOW Missile Launcher

Clan Membership History Improvements

  • The Clan History tab on the Player Info menu now shows the name of the Clan officer responsible for inviting or kicking the player.
